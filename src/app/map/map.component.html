Welcome to Geospatial Alerts and Advisories!

This application is a crowdsourced map of events. Events may be disasters, pest infestations, and other incidents related to climate and environmental issues. Users can choose to register to report these events, share images and videos, provide comments and feedback, and access useful summaries, visualizations, and some spatiotemporal analytics of the reported events.

Based on the reports, experts can also issue advisories to inform the public of possible effects of the event through the alerts and advisories module. The public can subscribe in the alerts and advisories module to receive updates and notifications.
